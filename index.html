Features

Everything a rider needs.
Nothing they don't.

🎙️

Hands-Free Voice Logging

Log fill-ups through your Sena, Cardo or any helmet communicator. Say the litres, cost and odometer — MotoFuel does the rest.

📊

Economy Tracking

Instant fuel economy readback after every fill-up. Track trends over time with beautiful charts. Know exactly how your bike is performing.

🛞

Tire Tracker

Manage multiple wheel sets, track tread depth and get wear predictions. Know when it's time for new rubber before you're miles from anywhere.

🏍️

Multiple Bikes

Track every bike in your garage separately. Road tyres on one, knobblies on the other — MotoFuel keeps it all organised.

🔒

Private by Default

All your data stays on your phone. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Just you and your bike.

🌍

Your Currency, Your Units

Supports any currency including ISK. Switch between metric and imperial. Available in English, Icelandic and Danish.

How it works

Fill up. Speak. Ride.

01

Say "Hey Siri, Log Fill-up"

Trigger MotoFuel hands-free through Siri or tap the mic button. Works perfectly through your helmet communicator while you're still at the pump.

02

Speak your fill-up details

MotoFuel asks for litres, cost and odometer one at a time. It reads back each value for confirmation — no gloves off, no screen squinting.

03

Say "Save" and hear your economy

MotoFuel saves everything and instantly reads back your fuel economy. Then get back on the road — the whole thing takes under 30 seconds.
