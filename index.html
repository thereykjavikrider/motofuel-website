What it does

Everything your
bike needs

One app. Fuel history, tire tracking, wheel sets, costs and predictions — all in one place.

⛽

Fuel Tracking

Log every fill-up — litres, cost, odometer. The app calculates L/100km or MPG automatically and shows you trends over time.

🛞

Wheel Sets

Manage multiple rim sets — road, trail, track. Swap with one tap. The app tracks km separately on each set and shows a visual history timeline.

📏

Tread Depth

Log tread readings over time. The app calculates your wear rate and predicts how many km remain before you hit the legal minimum.

📊

Burndown Prediction

After a few tire changes, the app learns the average lifespan of your preferred brand and model — and predicts when the next one will be due.

🏆

Hall of Fame

Every tire that goes the full distance earns its place. The Hall of Fame tracks your retired tires and crowns the all-time champion.

📈

Cost Trends

Track cost per km over time. Spot seasonal patterns, price changes and the impact of different riding styles on your wallet.

Bluetooth Logging

Gloves on.
Always.

Pull up to the pump, fill up, and log it — just your voice and your headset. MotoFuel works through your Sena or Cardo. It speaks, you answer. No need to touch your phone.

Tire Hall of Fame

Earned
every km

When a tire goes the full distance, it earns a place in the Hall of Fame. The app measures the final tread depth — if it's ≤ 2.0mm, the tire is officially retired and added to the prediction pool.

The more tires you retire properly, the smarter the burndown predictions become. Every worn-out tire makes the next prediction more accurate.
