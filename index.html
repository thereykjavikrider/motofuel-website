What it does

Everything your
bike needs

One app. Fuel history, tire tracking, GPS rides, live prices and Iron Butt certification — all in one place.

⛽

Fuel Tracking

Log every fill-up — litres, cost, odometer. Calculates L/100km or MPG automatically and shows economy trends over time.

🗺️

GPS Ride Tracking New

Every ride recorded. Route map, distance, duration, max and avg speed. Full ride history per bike with fill-up stops marked on the map.

⛽

Live Fuel Prices New

Nearby petrol stations with live prices shown on your route map. Iceland and UK covered. Find cheapest or nearest with one tap.

🏁

Iron Butt Mode New

Long distance certification mode. Receipt photos at every fuel stop, GPS route tracking and a PDF report ready for Iron Butt Association submission.

🛞

Tire Tracking

Manage multiple wheel sets. Swap with one tap. Track km per tire, tread depth over time and get burndown predictions.

📊

Cost Trends

Track cost per km over time. Spot seasonal patterns, price changes and the impact of riding style on your wallet.

📱

Lock Screen Live Activity New

Ride in progress shown on your lock screen with live timer. One tap logs a fill-up without unlocking your phone.

🏆

Hall of Fame

Every tire that goes the full distance earns its place. The more tires you retire, the smarter your burndown predictions become.

Bluetooth Logging

Gloves on.
Always.

Pull up to the pump, fill up, and log it — just your voice and your headset. MotoFuel works through your Sena or Cardo. It speaks, you answer. No need to touch your phone.

1

"Hey Siri, fill up in MotoFuel"

Siri shortcut launches the app instantly while you're still at the pump.

2

Which bike?

Say the name. The app recognises it even through wind noise.

3

Litres, cost, odometer

One question at a time. You speak the numbers. AI extracts them.

4

Ride type tagged

"On-road 70%, off-road 30%" — the app reads it back before saving.
