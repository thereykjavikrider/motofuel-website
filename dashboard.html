📱 How to export from the app

  1. Open MotoFuel on your iPhone
  2. Tap your bike → scroll down to the action buttons
  3. Tap the export icon (📤)
  4. Choose JSON format
  5. Save or AirDrop the file to your Mac
  6. Drop it here!
